# Weather Expert

The Weather Expert is a skill designed to assist users with all their weather-related queries. It can provide current weather conditions, forecasts for the next few days, and weather alerts. Here are some of the functionalities of the Weather Expert:

- Get current weather conditions
- Obtain forecasts for the next 3-5 days
- Receive weather alerts for severe weather conditions
- Provide advice based on weather conditions (e.g., dress appropriately for the weather)
